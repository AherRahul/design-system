# ProgressCircular

### ProgressCirculars are components that indicate the status/progress of an ongoing operation, in a circular format.
---
<br />

## When to use:
- It is necessary to convey the idea of ​​progress or percentage of an ongoing operation.
- It is necessary to convey the idea of ​​the status of an ongoing operation and what fraction of it has already been completed.
- It is necessary to draw the user's attention to specific information in a graphical way.

<br />

## Quanddo não usar:
- It is necessary to represent a fraction of a process with many steps.
