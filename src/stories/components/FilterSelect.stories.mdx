# FilterSelect

### FilterSelect allows users to choose options as filters for specific content. Derived from Selects, they have more specific implementation and applicability. They occupy less vertical space and do not have validation-related properties.
---
<br />

## When to use:
- When the user needs to choose a single option from a list with many items.
- When these options serve as filters for content on a page, card, or sidesheet, such as for maps, graphs, and reports.

<br />

## When not to use:
- As a replacement for the <a href="https://rds-design-system.netlify.app/?path=/docs/components-forms-select--select">Select</a> component.
- When the user needs to select more than one item from the list. In this case, use <a href="https://rds-design-system.netlify.app/?path=/docs/components-forms-multiselect--multiselect">Multiselects</a>.

<br />

## Observations:
- Place the most likely options to be selected at the top of the FilterSelect. When in doubt, sort them alphabetically.
- The labels of the selects should be succinct (one to three words).
