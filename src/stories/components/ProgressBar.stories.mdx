# ProgressBar

### ProgressBars are components used to indicate the status and progress of something to users.
---
<br />

## When to use:
- It is necessary to convey the idea of ​​progress in something.
- It is necessary to convey the idea of ​​the status of something.
- If you want to draw the user's attention to specific information in a graphical way.

<br />

## When not to use:
- It is necessary to create loaders.
- A lot of precision is required when showing numbers or progress.
- It is necessary to create steppers.
