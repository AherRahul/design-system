# Avatar

### Avatars are components used to display visual representations of users, such as images or names.
---
<br />

## When to use:
- The use of avatars is recommended when the user or list of users has defined images or names.
- It is generally used in conjunction with Headers, NavBars, Tables, Cards and Lists
