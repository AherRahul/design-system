# PageContainer

### PageContainers are basic structural blocks in RDS. They provide padding and content alignment.
---
<br />

## When to use:
- PageContainers must always be used when building pages, with the content being placed as a slot in the component.

<br />

## When not to use:
- Nested PageContainers should not be used.
