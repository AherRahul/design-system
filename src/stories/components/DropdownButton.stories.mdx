# DropdownButtons

### DropdownButtons are components used to activate popovers containing lists of actions.
---
<br />

## When to use:
- When you need to group actions under the same context.

<br />

## When not to use:
- Replacing a Multiselect or Select.
- To display filters
- With more than 5 actions

<br />
