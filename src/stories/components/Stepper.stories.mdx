# Stepper

### Steppers are components that control and display tasks divided into steps.

---
<br />

## When to use:
- It is necessary to visually show the status and current stage of a stepper.
- There is a modal with several steps.
- There are a series of entries or actions that want to show the progress of filling them, regardless of the date and time of the states.

<br />

## When not to use:
- If you need the chronology (date, time) of the steps. To do this, use the Timeline component.
- The order of steps, events or actions is not relevant.
