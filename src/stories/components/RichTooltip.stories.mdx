# RichTooltip

### RichTooltip, like Tooltips, are floating labels used to explain one of the interface, however they provide greater freedom for describing structured and/or longer texts.
---
<br />

## When to use:
- The text to be displayed as a label must occupy several lines or follow a certain structure, such as "label: descriptor".
- To display supplementary information about a feature or interface element.


<br />

## When not to use:
- With simple content, consisting of few words.
- To communicate errors and system status.
- To show critical information for understanding a feature or interface element.
